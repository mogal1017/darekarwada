AboutDarekar
Wada

welcome to darekar wada agri tourism, a peaceful place surrounded by greenery where you can enjoy fresh air and experience cow-based farming. we serve delicious rural cuisine cooked on traditional earthen chulha. it's a serene retreat for nature and animal lovers, offering diverse flora and fauna. we've been practicing ecological principles for nine years, supporting healthy farming, food systems, communities, and the environment. our focus is on sustainable living, and you can experience it firsthand by staying in our cozy earthen houses at chaitanya agro tourism. come and enjoy a harmonious weekend with nature!

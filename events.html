Welcome to Darekar wada

Welcome to Darekar Wada Agro Tourism, where responsible tourism, entertainment, and eco-friendly practices coexist harmoniously. Experience rural life through farming, animal husbandry, and bullock cart riding while learning about sustainability. We aim to spread joy while preserving the environment and supporting local communities. Join us in embracing a greener, more harmonious world.
